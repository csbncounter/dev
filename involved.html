Pray with Us

Prayer is first among our needs.

This is God’s event for the encouragement and unity of our Regions and Battalions, not to mention an intensely fun, deeply spiritual experience for our guys. As such, only God can accomplish this. Please join with us in prayer, specifically by registering for the N-Counter Newsletter. Each month you will hear up to date prayer requests and praise reports.

Donate

Support our physical needs

The N-Counter Steering Committee wants to see every Battalion attending this event. To ensure all can attend, the Committee is committed to fundraising in order to offset a portion of the cost for units and families. Fundraising is also necessary to solidify reservations, bookings, and administrative concerns in a timely manner.

Strategically, every battalion unit is being asked to create its own funding plan well in advance of the event. We recommend a plan in which your unit raises funds together to a certain level, and then encourages each battalion member or camper in how to pay their individual remaining balance.

Perhaps you are familiar with Christian Service Brigade and its mission. Maybe you have been influenced by godly men who mentored you. We invite your participation and involvement. Can you join us in some way? Can you lend your financial support to this event with a sizeable contribution? Please follow Donate and look for Special Events 520 N-Counter 2016.
